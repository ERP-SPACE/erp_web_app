 Executive Summary This ERP is designed to manage the end-to-end operations of a sublimation and speciality paper trading business. It integrates purchase workflows, landed cost allocations, roll-level inventory management, barcode-based traceability, customer onboarding, credit management, WhatsApp-driven sales, and full-fledged accounting. The ERP replaces fragmented spreadsheets, WhatsApp orders, and external accounting tools (such as Tally) with a unified, scalable platform . Phase 1 focuses on Gujarat (specifically the Surat hub), but the system is designed for a pan-India rollout. 1. Objectives & KPI Targets 1.1 Business Objectives & Goals: One source of truth for all purchases, stock, sales, and accounts. Automated workflows to minimise manual effort. Automate purchase-to-ledger and sales-to-ledger flows. Automate purchase ? inward ? invoice ? accounting with landed cost. Streamline sales flow : SO ? DC ? SI in 2 clicks per stage . Deep traceability to manage quality issues and vendor accountability. Track stock roll-by-roll with QR/barcodes, batch, and vendor and customer mapping. Customer-centric pricing: enforce 44&quot; benchmark with rate history and special order deals rates. Manage customer rates, discounts, credit days, and grace days with auto-blocking. Intelligent credit control with auto-block and WhatsApp dunning. Provide analytics dashboards for stock, sales, credit, and profitability. Enable WhatsApp automation for orders, statements, invoices, outstandings and reminders. Replace Tally with integrated accounting (ledgers, GST, trial balance, P&L, balance sheet). Accounting built-in: auto vouchers, financial statements, GST. Scalable architecture for new categories and geographies. Scalable catalogue (sublimation, butter paper, future categories). 1.2 KPIs = 99.9% of rolls with valid barcode lineage. = 24 hrs average age of Unmapped rolls. = 90% orders initiated/confirmed via WhatsApp. Real-time P&L and Balance Sheet (= 5 minutes data freshness). Zero manual journal adjustments are required in normal operations. 2. Glossary Roll: Physical roll of paper with width (inches) and length (meters). SKU: Sellable variant defined by hierarchy: GSM ? Quality ? Size(Width) . 44ö Benchmark: All customer rates are anchored at 44ö; other widths derived by ratio unless overridden. Unmapped Roll: Inwarded roll with incomplete attribute mapping to SKU. Mapped Roll: Inwarded roll fully linked to a SKU. GRN: Goods Receipt Note (partial allowed). DC: Delivery Challan. PI/SI: Purchase/Sales Invoice. Landed Cost: Base price + freight + duties + clearing + misc + GST, allocated to rolls. Credit Days & Grace Days: Grace days are internal only (not exposed to the customer). 3. Algorithms & Calculations Derived Pricing (Mandatory 44ö) derived_rate_for_width(W) = base_rate_44 ū (W / 44) then round (nearest ?1 configurable). - Override : allowed at SO/SI with reason + approval if over tolerance (configurable e.g., &gt;5% deviation ? manager approval). Discount Application - Line discount applied before tax; Bill discount allocated proportionally by line value for GST correctness. - Rounding: paise handled using ōlargest remainderö method to ensure invoice total integrity. Landed Cost Allocation - Bases: ROLL , METER , VALUE . - Formula per roll: allocation = total_cost ū (roll_basis / sum_all_roll_basis) ; update landed_cost_per_roll . - Reallocation triggers revaluation events; cost of goods for already dispatched rolls not retro-changed (post to variance ledger if required). COGS on Invoice - COGS = sum(landed_cost_per_roll ū number_of_rolls_billed) with partial meters handled by proportional cost if partial returns exist. - Inventory credit equals COGS debit. Credit Blocking - Compute exposure = outstanding_AR + value_of_pending_SO . - Block when: - Rule OVER_LIMIT: exposure &gt; credit_limit. - Rule OVER_DUE: any invoice overdue by (credit_days + grace_days). - BOTH: either condition. - On block ? SO status OnHold ; WhatsApp notice to customer + internal. - Admin override requires reason, captured in AuditLog. GST Determination - Intra-state: CGST+SGST; Inter-state: IGST; rates from SKU.tax_rate or overrides. - HSN configurable per category; appear on invoices. 9.
